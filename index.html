ABOUT BRUNO PIZZERIA

Founded by Mr. Bruno, an Italian who is a second-generation pizza maker of the family, Bruno Pizzeria guarantees to bring the taste of not only traditional pizza but also the modern ones. We are proud to say that our pizza, made from the best materials imported from Italy, are the best in Melbourne. Coming to Bruno Pizzeria, you will experience the atmostphere of an Italian store and smell and taste the best pizza in the world.

We also provide delivery service, so customer can enjoy our pizza while staying at home. We guarantee to bring you the food in the best condition within less than an hour.

OUR MISSION

We aim to become the largest pizza store in Melbourne which only serves the best products at the best price. We also aim to build and develop the best customer service system, meaning that customer's experience is our top priority.
